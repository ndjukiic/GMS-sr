Manpower Europa D.O.O.

At Manpower Europa D.O.O., we make sure the right people find the right jobs, no matter where in the world they're from.

Manpower Europa D.O.O. operates in Serbia, with an aim to employ people from all around the world, in open construction vacancies of various Serbian companies, anywhere across the country.

  • We'll support you through the process of your Serbian Visa application.

  • We'll process all the paperwork for you.

  • We'll handle travel logistics to whatever destination you're getting to.

  • We'll take care of accommodation.

  • We'll help you integrate in your local community, language & culture.

  • We keep track of your feedback, so we can do better.

Our workers feel honored to work in Serbia, where most of them came from Asia...

...while all of them are open to work in any remedy of construction industry.
